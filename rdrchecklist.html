I. The Bankroll

Sheriff's desk in the burned town. Buy map from Maximo (West of Flatneck). Essential for Satchel crafting. Unlocks Fast Travel.

II. The Arsenal

Valentine Doctor's Back Room. Rhodes Gunsmith basement. Watson's Cabin basement (North of Strawberry). Mission: "The First Shall Be Last". Kill LEFT bounty hunter. Hermit's Shack (Manito Glade).

III. Bandit Challenges (Dishonor)

Aim and rob in Valentine. Sell to Emerald Ranch fence. Valentine, Strawberry, Rhodes, Saint Denis. Ride fast. Camp near Emerald Ranch and farm wagons. Go on a rampage in one state.

IV. Explorer Challenges (Cash)

Maximo near Flatneck Station. Complete the 3-part chain. 2 Gold Bars. Find the random explorer on a cliff. 3 Gold Bars. Start at Cairn Lake. 4 Gold Bars.

V. Master Hunter (Satchels)

Basic food source. Use Varmint Rifle. Essential for crafting. Use binoculars actively. Aim, press Square/X to call, headshot. Grizzly or Black Bear. North of Strawberry. Poison Arrows. North of Owanjila.

VI. Herbalist (Tonics)

Found in Heartlands grassy areas. Raspberry, Blackberry, Evergreen, Wintergreen. Potent Bitters or Seasoned Meat. Parasol mushrooms are common.

VII. Sharpshooter (Dead Eye)

Any bird works. Bird + Rabbit, or Deer + Coyote. Ride on the roof. Don't get caught. Use Homing Tomahawk on a stationary guard.

VIII. Horseman (Riding)

Run them over or shoot them. Find a fence line. Ride fast. Use stimulants. Lasso someone and ride in circles.

IX. The Legend of the East Satchel

Craft the Trinket first. 99 Inventory slots.
